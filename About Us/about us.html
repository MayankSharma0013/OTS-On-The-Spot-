About Us

OTS (On The Spot)

Let's face it . We all know that owning a car is much simpler than maintaining it .
Car service and repair is not only time consuming but also quite expensive . Top of it most of us do not even know what we are paying for or how much time will the repair take . Our lack of automotive knowledge is often taken advantage of by local or authorised workshops and all we end up with is high bills , low quality repairs and a horrible experience .

At OTS , we believe that car service should be convenient , transparent , high quality and reliable . We understand that car is a complex machine and it is difficult for you to figure out the correct solution for your car's symptoms . This is where our skilled and knowledgeable in house technicians come to your rescue . Our well trained technician come right at your doorstep to diagnose the problem and suggest or book the correct service .

Minor repairs or regular service are either done at the comfort of your doorstep or at OTS , certified garage near your location . We also provide free pickup and drop facility for you to avoid the dreadful traffic . You are given the quote even before any service or repair work starts . To put it even better we provide service to your car only post your approval . You also receive timely service updates and post service support whenever needed.

Today we don't have many customers but we believe it's just the beginning...
